Rules

  • After starting the game the you will have the Hit button to get a card.
  • You can again click the hit button to get another card.
  • Your goal is to get the highest score but not higher than 21. If your score is more than 21 than you are busted.
  • Cards 1-9 will have the value of their equivalent numbers, cards J, Q and K will have a value of 10.
  • The value of A can be 1 or 10 depending on the situation (if the score is + 10 >= 21 then A will be counted as 10 and if the score is + 10< 21 then A will be counted as 1.
  • If you think you don't want to get another card, then you click the stand button.
  • Stand button will allow dealer to show his cards.
  • The dealer's goal is also to get more points than you, but if the dealer's score is less than 16 he must take another card and if his score is greater than 16 he cannot take any more cards.
  • If no one is busted then the person with the highest score will be the winner.
  • If one of you is busted then the other will be the winner because his score is less than 21
  • If both of you are busted or you both have the same score then it will be a draw.
